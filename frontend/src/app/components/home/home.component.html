About Altius 2k25

Altius 2k25 is the premier technical fest organized to showcase innovation, creativity, and technical prowess. This year, we bring together students from various departments to compete in exciting technical events, workshops, and competitions. Join us in this celebration of technology and innovation!

With a diverse range of events spanning coding competitions, robotics challenges, design contests, and more, Altius 2k25 promises to be an unforgettable experience for all participants. Our goal is to foster learning, collaboration, and healthy competition among the brightest minds.
